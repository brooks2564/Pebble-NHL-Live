# NHL Live Scores 🏒

Can't watch your favorite team's game? Now you don't have to — strap it to your wrist.

NHL Live Scores is a Pebble watchface that keeps you up to date on live NHL action without ever pulling out your phone.

## What It Does

- **Live scores** with team abbreviations in team colors
- **Period & time remaining** for the current period
- **Shots on goal** for both teams
- **Power play indicator** — know when your team is on the man advantage
- **Last goal scorer** displayed in real time
- **Team records** (W-L-OTL) for both teams
- **Goal celebration** — animated goal light flashes when someone scores
- **8-bit icons** — stick & puck during play, zamboni during intermission
- **Playoff series status** — shows series record instead of season record during playoffs
- **Next game** — when no game is live, shows the next scheduled matchup
- **Battery bar** at the bottom of the screen
- **Scrolling ticker** of other games around the league

## Supported Platforms

Aplite, Basalt, Diorite, Emery, Flint
